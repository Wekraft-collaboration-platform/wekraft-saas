# Sprints

Sprints help teams focus on a specific set of work over a fixed period, typically 1 or 2 weeks.

## Managing Sprints
- **Backlog**: The pool of all pending tasks.
- **Sprint Planning**: Moving tasks from the backlog into the active sprint.
- **Burndown Chart**: A visual representation of work remaining versus time.

## Sprint Insights
Wekraft provides AI-driven predictions for your sprint:
- **Velocity Tracking**: How much work your team actually completes.
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify tasks that are unlikely to be finished by the end of the sprint.
