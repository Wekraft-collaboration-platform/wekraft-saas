# Manage Teams

Administrative tools for team leads and owners.

## Roles and Permissions
- **Owner**: Full access to billing and team settings.
- **Admin**: Can manage projects and members.
- **Member**: Can create and work on tasks.
- **Guest**: Limited access to specific projects.

## Inviting Members
Invite users via email or share a join link for your organization. You can also set up SSO (Single Sign-On) for larger enterprises.
