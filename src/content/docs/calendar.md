# Calendar

The calendar provides a temporal view of your projects and personal schedule.

## Views
- **Monthly**: High-level view of deadlines and milestones.
- **Weekly**: Detailed breakdown of daily tasks.
- **Project Calendar**: Filter the view to show only items from a specific project.

## Syncing
Wekraft supports syncing with external calendars via iCal links, so you can see your tasks alongside your meetings.
