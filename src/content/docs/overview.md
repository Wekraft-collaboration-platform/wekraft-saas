# Wekraft Overview

Welcome to **Wekraft**, the next-generation collaboration platform designed to bridge the gap between project management and development.

## What is Wekraft?
Wekraft is a unified workspace where teams can manage projects, track tasks, and sync their development workflow directly with their IDE.

### Key Value Propositions
- **Integrated Workflow**: No more switching between browser and IDE.
- **AI-Driven Insights**: Get predictive analytics on member workload and project health.
- **Real-time Collaboration**: Instant updates across your entire team.

## How it Works
1. **Create a Project**: Set up your workspace and invite your team.
2. **Sync GitHub**: Connect your repositories to track commits and PRs.
3. **Install Extension**: Bring your tasks into VS Code.
4. **Iterate**: Use sprints and issues to move fast.
